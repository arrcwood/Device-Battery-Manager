## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I add a device to track its battery?

1. Open the app  
2. Tap **Add Device**  
3. Enter the device name (e.g., “Living Room Remote”)  
4. Select the battery type it uses  
5. Save your device  

You'll now be able to track when its battery was last replaced.

### How do I update a battery replacement?

1. Select the device from your list  
2. Tap **Replace Battery**  
3. Confirm the replacement date  

The app will update the device’s history and reset the timer.

### Can I track multiple devices?

Absolutely! Device Battery Manager allows you to track as many household devices as you’d like.

### Is the app free to use?

Yes! Device Battery Manager is completely free to download and use. The app may include non-personalized advertising to support development.

We'll investigate and resolve the issue as quickly as possible.
